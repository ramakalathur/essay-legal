Privacy Policy

Essay - UPSC CSE Mains

Effective date: February 27, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Essay app handles your information.

Essay is designed to work primarily on-device for reading question papers, answers, outlines, favorites, highlights, and unread tracking.

2. Data Collected

We do not require account sign-up and do not collect personal profile data for app access.

The app currently stores the following data on your device:

  • Favorite questions
  • Read/viewed question status
  • Text highlights created by you
  • UI preferences (for example, compact/expanded years mode)
  • Tip UI state counters (demo mode)

3. How Data Is Used

Stored data is used only to provide app functionality such as showing favorites, unread indicators, highlights, and personalized UI state.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data.

We do not share your personal data with third parties for advertising.

As of this version, no third-party analytics or ad SDKs are integrated.

5. Payments

Optional tips are processed through Apple In-App Purchase (StoreKit) as consumable purchases.

Payment information is handled by Apple under Apple's policies. The app does not collect or store your card or bank details and only uses Apple-provided purchase status/details required to complete the tip flow in-app.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Because data is stored locally on your device, you control it directly.

  • You can remove favorites/highlights by using in-app controls.
  • Uninstalling the app removes locally stored app data from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

The app is intended for exam preparation users and is not specifically directed to children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when app features change. The effective date at the top will be revised accordingly.
