Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I access a year paper?
    On the home screen, tap any year card or year in the compact carousel.
  • How do favorites work?
    Open a question answer and tap the star icon. Favorites appear on the home screen and in the Favorites list.
  • How do highlights work?
    Select text inside Essay or Outline and use the highlight action. Highlights are saved on your device.
  • Can I search across all years?
    Yes. Use Search from the Years card header to search by year, question number, or topic keywords.
  • Will I lose my data if I uninstall the app?
    Yes. Favorites, highlights, and read status are stored locally on your device and are removed if the app is uninstalled.
